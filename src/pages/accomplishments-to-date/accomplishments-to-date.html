What is WATOG

Achievements to date

Since 2012, WATOG has promoted 13 new associations for trainees and/or young OB/GYN globally.
Mexico (2012): AMEJOG
Tunisia (2012): TATOG
Zambia (2012): Within ZAGO
Zimbabwe (2013): ZATOG
Nepal (2013): Within NESOG
Salvador (2013): Within ASOGOES
Bolivia (2013): Within SBGOB
Nigeria (2013): WATOG-Nigeria, within SOGON
Egypt (2014): Within ESGO
Russia (2014): Within RSOG
Japan (2015): JTOG within JSOG
Burkina Faso (2015): ABIRGO
South Africa (2016): SAATOG
Survey/Educational Platform Building:
In 2016 a first survey on OB/GYN Ultrasound training was issued in Europe with the collaboration of ENTOG.
The results were published in the UOG Journal in 2017 In 2017 a first global survey was conducted on the same subject and the preliminary results presented at the ISUOG World Congress in Vienna.
