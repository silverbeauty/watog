Futur Near-Term Goals

Network Building
Furthering two years of successful co-operation with leaders in ultrasound training we are aiming to establish similar collaboration with other leaders of OB/GYN sub-specialties.
WATOG Online Campus
Primarily a tool for senior societies to disseminate the educational contents; WATOG is designing a web-based platform freely accessible for trainees in order to facilitate networking and information exchange.
Trainee Exchange Program
The ENTOG/Scandinavian network facilitates Trainee Exchange Programs; however WATOG is aiming to expand the sporadically organized exchange programs in non-Europe regions by facilitating bi- and multilateral programs based on the IFMSA program examples.
