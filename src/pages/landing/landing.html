What is WATOG

The World Association of Trainees in Obstetrics and Gynecology (WATOG) is a non-profit organization that represents the worldwide network of young obstetricians and gynecologists (OB/GYN)

Mission of WATOG

WATOG's main mission is to help every OB/GYN trainee worldwide to access the highest level of education, overcome cultural and gender barriers and facilitate contact and exchange between members globally.

Organisation

Founded in 2012 under the auspices of the International Federation of Gynaecologists and Obstetricians and with the support of major international senior societies, WATOG started as an idea from two young doctors in France - to create a world association for OB/GYN trainees.

Accomplishments to date

Since 2012, WATOG has promoted 13 new associations for trainees and/or young OB/GYN globally.
